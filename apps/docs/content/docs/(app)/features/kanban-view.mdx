---
title: Kanban View
description: Manage workspace status, priority, and labels on a board when running many tasks.
---

Kanban view turns your workspaces into a board—columns for status, labels for theme, and priority when you need to decide what to pick up next.

## Why it matters

- See several agent or human tasks at a glance.
- Spot blocked or stale work quickly.
- Coordinate parallel workspaces without a separate PM tool.
- Drag cards instead of hunting through lists.

## What you can track

- Workspace status (active, blocked, done, and custom columns).
- Priority when multiple tasks compete.
- Labels for area, repo, or agent type.

## Get started

1. Open **Kanban** from Atmos.
2. Place each workspace in the right column.
3. Add labels or priority where helpful.
4. Open a card to jump into that workspace.

## Tips

- Answer one question with the board: “What should I work on next?”
- Do not over-tag—lightweight columns beat perfect taxonomy.
